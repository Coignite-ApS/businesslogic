**Last updated:** 2026-03-24

## GTM Model: Developer-First PLG + Content-Led Growth

### Why PLG
- Product is self-serve (calculator builder, API, SDK)
- ACV under $10K → PLG achieves 3-5x lower CAC
- Free tier enables zero-friction evaluation
- Developer SDK creates natural PLG funnel

### Why Content-Led
- "How to build a calculator" = high-intent search queries
- "AI knowledge base for business" = growing search volume
- Technical content (MCP guides, API tutorials) attracts developer audience
- SEO compounds over time — each article is a permanent acquisition channel

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary: "Excel Expert"
- **Who:** Consultants, financial advisors, insurance brokers, accountants
- **Company size:** 1-50 employees
- **Trigger:** "Can you put that calculator on our website?"
- **Budget:** $19-249/mo (marketing/operations)
- **Decision maker:** Owner/founder
- **Where they are:** LinkedIn, industry forums, AppSumo, Google search

### Secondary: "API Builder"
- **Who:** SaaS developers needing formula eval or AI KB
- **Company size:** 5-200 employees (tech)
- **Trigger:** Building feature requiring calculations or AI answers
- **Budget:** $79-249/mo or enterprise
- **Decision maker:** CTO/Lead dev
- **Where they are:** GitHub, npm, HackerNews, dev blogs, Discord

### Anti-ICP (Avoid)
- Large enterprises wanting Tableau replacement (long sales cycle, wrong product)
- Consumers wanting personal finance calculators (low LTV)
- Companies wanting generic chatbots (not our strength)
