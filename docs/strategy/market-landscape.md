- Estimated **$50M-200M** (emerging niche at intersection of calculators + AI + embedded analytics)

## Buyer Profiles

### Primary: "Excel Expert" (Non-Technical Business Professional)
- Insurance agents, financial advisors, consultants, accountants
- Builds spreadsheets with proprietary calculations
- Wants to embed on website without developer help
- Budget: $19-249/mo from operations/marketing budget
- Evaluates on: ease of use, embedding quality, design flexibility

### Secondary: "API Builder" (Developer/Technical)
- SaaS developers, integrators, agencies
- Needs formula evaluation or AI KB in their product
- Evaluates SDK, docs, API reliability
- Budget: $79-249/mo or enterprise custom
- Evaluates on: API quality, documentation, reliability, pricing predictability

### Tertiary: "AI-First Business" (Forward-Looking)
- Companies wanting MCP-accessible business intelligence
- Early adopters deploying AI agents in workflows
- Budget: $249+/mo (enterprise)
- Evaluates on: MCP support, AI capabilities, integration breadth

## Technology Shifts

1. **AI-native tools replacing traditional BI** — Users expect conversational interfaces, not dashboards
2. **MCP as universal AI connectivity** — Becoming the standard for AI agent ↔ tool communication
3. **Embedded > standalone** — 75% of enterprises will embed AI-oriented analytics in business apps by 2026
4. **Credit-based pricing dominant** — 79 companies in PricingSaaS 500 Index offer credits (126% YoY growth)
5. **Inference costs plummeting** — 50x/year median decline. Margin-positive AI features now viable for SMB pricing.
